About

My name is Ford Fowler, and I'm an aspiring web developer. I've managed to reach the critical age of 24 in much the same way most people do: stumbling blindly. My childhood was split between Colorado and Southern California, both outdoor wonderlands for a kid. Fall of 2010, I went to college at UC Berkeley, but quickly withdrew to take time to discover what I actually wanted to do. Later I studied and researched Geology at Colorado State University, but after taking a few programming classes a strong hankering to learn coding engulfed me. When I discovered Code Fellows I jumped, packed my car, and shipped my life to Seattle.
